## 🌟 Getting Started

Welcome to PlantStore! This guide will help you navigate our plant e-commerce platform and make the most of your shopping experience.

### Accessing PlantStore

1. **Open your web browser** and navigate to the PlantStore website
2. **Browse products** without creating an account, or
3. **Create an account** for full functionality including cart, orders, and reviews

### System Requirements

- **Modern web browser** (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- **Internet connection** for full functionality
- **JavaScript enabled** in your browser

## 👤 Account Management

### Creating an Account

1. Click **"Sign Up"** in the top navigation
2. Fill in the registration form:
   - **First Name**: Your first name
   - **Last Name**: Your last name
   - **Email**: A valid email address (used for login)
   - **Password**: At least 6 characters
3. Click **"Create Account"**
4. You'll be automatically logged in after successful registration

### Logging In

1. Click **"Login"** in the top navigation
2. Enter your **email** and **password**
3. Click **"Sign In"**
4. You'll be redirected to the homepage

### Logging Out

1. Click on your **profile icon** in the top navigation
2. Select **"Logout"** from the dropdown menu

## 🌱 Browsing Products

### Homepage

The homepage displays:

- **Featured products** in a grid layout
- **Category navigation** bar below the main navigation
- **Search functionality** in the header

### Product Categories

1. Use the **category bar** below the main navigation to filter products
2. Available categories include:
   - Indoor Plants
   - Outdoor Plants
   - Succulents
   - And more...

### Product Search

1. Click on the **search icon** in the header
2. Enter product name or keywords
3. Press **Enter** or click the search button
4. Browse filtered results

### Product Details

Click on any product card to view:

- **High-resolution product images**
- **Detailed descriptions**
- **Pricing information**
- **Stock availability**
- **Customer reviews**
- **Related products**

### Product Information

Each product page shows:

- **Product name** and description
- **Current price**
- **Stock status** (In Stock/Out of Stock)
- **Category information**
- **Customer ratings** and reviews
- **Add to Cart** button

## 🛒 Shopping Cart

### Adding Items to Cart

1. **Navigate to a product** page
2. Click **"Add to Cart"** button
3. The item is added with a quantity of 1
4. A **confirmation message** appears

> **Note**: You must be logged in to add items to your cart.

### Viewing Your Cart

1. Click the **cart icon** in the top navigation
2. The cart shows:
   - **Product images** and names
   - **Individual prices**
   - **Quantity selectors**
   - **Subtotal** for each item
   - **Total cart value**

### Managing Cart Items

#### Updating Quantities

1. In your cart, use the **quantity selector** (+ and - buttons)
2. Changes are saved automatically
3. Totals update in real-time

#### Removing Items

1. Click the **"Remove"** button next to any item
2. Confirm the removal when prompted
3. The item is immediately removed from your cart

### Cart Summary

The cart displays:

- **Subtotal**: Total of all items
- **Estimated shipping**: Calculated based on location
- **Total**: Final amount including shipping

## 📦 Placing Orders

### Checkout Process

1. **Review your cart** and ensure all items are correct
2. Click **"Proceed to Checkout"**
3. **Add shipping address** (if not already saved)
4. **Review order summary**
5. **Complete payment** through Stripe

### Shipping Address

#### Adding a New Address

1. During checkout, click **"Add New Address"**
2. Fill in the address form:
   - **First Name** and **Last Name**
   - **Street Address**
   - **City**
   - **Postal Code**
   - **Country**
3. Choose to **save as default** (optional)
4. Click **"Save Address"**

#### Using Saved Addresses

1. Select from **existing addresses** in the dropdown
2. Click **"Use This Address"**

### Payment

PlantStore uses **Stripe** for secure payment processing:

1. **Review your order** summary
2. Click **"Pay Now"**
3. You'll be redirected to **Stripe's secure checkout**
4. Enter your **payment information**:
   - Card number
   - Expiration date
   - CVC code
   - Billing address
5. Click **"Complete Payment"**

#### Supported Payment Methods

- **Credit Cards**: Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- **Debit Cards**: Most major debit cards
- **Digital Wallets**: Apple Pay, Google Pay (where available)

### Order Confirmation

After successful payment:

1. You'll see an **order confirmation** page
2. **Order details** are displayed including:
   - Order number
   - Items purchased
   - Shipping address
   - Total amount paid
3. An **email confirmation** is sent to your registered email

## 📋 Order Management

### Viewing Order History

1. **Click your profile icon** in the top navigation
2. Select **"My Orders"**
3. View a list of all your orders with:
   - **Order number**
   - **Order date**
   - **Status** (Pending, Processing, Shipped, Delivered)
   - **Total amount**

### Order Details

Click on any order to view:

- **Detailed item list** with quantities and prices
- **Shipping address**
- **Order status** and tracking information
- **Payment status**
- **Order timeline**

### Order Status Meanings

- **Pending**: Order placed, awaiting processing
- **Processing**: Order is being prepared for shipment
- **Shipped**: Order has been shipped and is on its way
- **Delivered**: Order has been successfully delivered
- **Cancelled**: Order was cancelled (rare)

### Order Issues

If you have issues with your order:

1. **Check the order status** first
2. **Contact customer support** through the contact form
3. **Include your order number** in any communication

## ⭐ Product Reviews

### Writing Reviews

After purchasing a product, you can leave a review:

1. **Navigate to the product page**
2. Scroll to the **"Reviews"** section
3. Click **"Write a Review"**
4. Fill in the review form:
   - **Rating**: 1-5 stars
   - **Written review**: Your experience with the product
5. Click **"Submit Review"**

> **Note**: You can only review products you have purchased.

### Review Guidelines

When writing reviews:

- **Be honest** about your experience
- **Include helpful details** about the plant's condition, size, etc.
- **Be respectful** in your language
- **Focus on the product** rather than shipping or service issues

### Reading Reviews

Product reviews show:

- **Star rating** (1-5 stars)
- **Written review** from the customer
- **Reviewer name** (first name only)
- **Review date**
- **Verified purchase** indicator

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Can't Log In

**Problem**: Error message when trying to log in
**Solutions**:

1. **Check email and password** for typos
2. **Use "Forgot Password"** if you can't remember
3. **Clear browser cache** and cookies
4. **Try a different browser**

#### Cart Items Disappearing

**Problem**: Items removed from cart unexpectedly
**Solutions**:

1. **Log in to your account** (guest carts are temporary)
2. **Check if items are out of stock**
3. **Refresh the page**

#### Payment Issues

**Problem**: Payment fails during checkout
**Solutions**:

1. **Verify card information** is correct
2. **Check with your bank** for any blocks
3. **Try a different payment method**
4. **Contact customer support**

#### Slow Loading

**Problem**: Website loads slowly
**Solutions**:

1. **Check internet connection**
2. **Clear browser cache**
3. **Disable browser extensions** temporarily
4. **Try a different browser**

#### Order Status Not Updating

**Problem**: Order appears stuck in one status
**Solutions**:

1. **Allow 24-48 hours** for status updates
2. **Check email** for notifications
3. **Contact customer support** with order number
