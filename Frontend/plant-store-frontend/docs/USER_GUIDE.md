# User Guide

Welcome to the Plant Store! This guide will help you navigate and use all the features of our online plant store.

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. **Register**: Click the "Login" button in the top navigation and select "Register"
2. **Fill in Details**: Provide your email, password, and personal information
3. **Address Information**: Add your default shipping address during registration
4. **Verification**: Check your email for verification (if required)

### Logging In

1. Click the "Login" button in the navigation bar
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In" to access your account

## Browsing Products

### Homepage

- View featured plants and special offers
- Browse by popular categories
- See new arrivals and bestsellers

### Product Categories

- Use the category bar below the main navigation
- Click on category icons to filter products
- Categories include: Indoor Plants, Outdoor Plants, Succulents, Herbs, etc.

### Product Search

1. Use the search bar in the top navigation
2. Type plant names, categories, or keywords
3. Results appear as you type
4. Click on suggestions or press Enter to search

### Product Filtering

Use the sidebar filters to narrow your search:

- **Price Range**: Set minimum and maximum price
- **Availability**: Show only in-stock items
- **Category**: Filter by plant category
- **Rating**: Filter by customer ratings

## Product Details

### Viewing Products

Each product page includes:

- **High-quality images**: Multiple photos of the plant
- **Detailed description**: Care instructions and plant information
- **Price and stock status**: Current pricing and availability
- **Customer reviews**: Ratings and comments from other buyers
- **Care guide**: Watering, light, and maintenance instructions

### Adding to Cart

1. Select quantity using the +/- buttons
2. Click "Add to Cart"
3. View confirmation message
4. Continue shopping or proceed to cart

## Shopping Cart

### Managing Your Cart

- **View Cart**: Click the cart icon in the navigation
- **Update Quantities**: Use +/- buttons next to each item
- **Remove Items**: Click the remove (X) button
- **View Total**: See subtotal, taxes, and shipping costs

### Cart Drawer

- Quick access slide-out panel
- Shows cart items and total
- Direct checkout button
- Update quantities without leaving current page

## Checkout Process

### Step 1: Review Cart Items

- Verify all items and quantities
- Apply discount codes if available
- Review total cost breakdown

### Step 2: Shipping Information

- Select delivery address
- Add new address if needed
- Choose delivery method:
  - Standard Delivery (5-7 business days)
  - Express Delivery (2-3 business days)
  - Next Day Delivery (metropolitan areas)

### Step 3: Payment

Choose your payment method:

- **Credit/Debit Card**: Visa, Mastercard, American Express
- **Digital Wallets**: PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay
- **Buy Now, Pay Later**: Available payment plans

### Order Confirmation

- Review all details before placing order
- Click "Place Order" to complete purchase
- Receive confirmation email with order details

## Account Management

### Profile Settings

- **Personal Information**: Update name, email, phone

### Address Book

## Troubleshooting

### Common Website Issues

- **Slow Loading**: Check internet connection, clear browser cache
- **Cart Problems**: Refresh page, check if items are still in stock
- **Login Issues**: Reset password, check email for verification
- **Payment Errors**: Verify card information, try different payment method

### Plant Care Emergencies

- **Wilting Plants**: Check watering schedule and light conditions
- **Yellow Leaves**: May indicate overwatering or nutrient issues
- **Pest Problems**: Identify common pests and treatment options
- **Seasonal Changes**: Adjust care routine for different seasons
